Good Eggs

Simple yet everything. It doesn't take much to turn plain eggs into this masterpiece of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Pink Himalayan or Black salt
  • Pepper
  • Green onion
  • Garlic
  • Dill
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder

Instructions

  1. Sautee the green onions and garlic in butter.
  2. Mix eggs in bow with salt, pepper, dill, onion powder and garlic powder.
  3. When butter is aromatic, scramble eggs in the butter.
  4. Add optional cheese and/or siracha.
  5. Most importantly, enjoy!
