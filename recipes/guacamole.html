Good Guacamole

Simple ingredients come together to get an party started.

Ingredients

  • 6 avocados
  • 4 roma tomatoes
  • 2 limes
  • 1/2 onion or 1 cup of onion
  • ~1-1.5 tbsp salt (pink himilayan preferred
  • 3-4 gloves of garlic minced
  • 1/4 - 1/2 cup cilantro (the more the better)

Instructions

  1. Pit and smash the avocados. Put in bowl.
  2. Dice tomatoes and onion and add to bowl.
  3. Mince garlic and add to bowl.
  4. Squeeze lime juice into bowl.
  5. Add salt (crucial! the lime and salt are nothing without eachother)
  6. Chop cilantro and add to bowl.
  7. Mix and most importantly, enjoy!
