Good Chesapeake Burger

The ultimate "surf and turf" burger. Deliciously seasoned chunky crab atop a juicy beef patty.

Ingredients

  • Blue crabmeat
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Mustard
    • Fresh parsely
  • Beef Patty
  • Bun
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce

Instructions

  1. Sautee garlic in butter.
  2. Add mustard, Old Bay, and parsely to butter.
  3. Add crab to butter. Cook a little and turn off heat.
  4. Grill a beef patty with some Lowry's season salt.
  5. Get your bun and burger tops. Make a burger and add crab!
  6. Most importantly, enjoy!
